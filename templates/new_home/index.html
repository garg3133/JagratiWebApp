About Us

You must not lose faith in humanity. Humanity is an ocean; if a few drops of the ocean are dirty, the ocean does not become dirty.

VISION

Our vision is to promote education as a vehicle for rural development and provide it to students who cannot otherwise access or afford it. By empowering children with education, we want to make sure that they live out their potential even under adverse circumstances. And light the future of generations to come.

MISSION

Our mission is to eradicate illiteracy from India. We are moving towards our mission by implementing this in IIIT Jabalpur, giving quality and value education to students of nearby villages. Our ultimate goal is to extend our helping hand to each and every needy in India and make a new India in the coming days.
